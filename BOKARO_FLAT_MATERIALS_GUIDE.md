# Bokaro Steel Plant - Flat Surface Materials Guide

## Overview
Bokaro Steel Plant specializes in **flat surface materials only**. All pages have been updated to reflect this product portfolio.

---

## Material Types & Specifications

### 1. **CR Coils (Cold Rolled Coils)**
- **Thickness**: 0.5 - 3.0 mm
- **Width**: 600 - 1500 mm
- **Length**: Coil form
- **Density**: 7.85 g/cm³
- **Applications**: Automotive, appliances, packaging
- **Quality**: High surface finish, excellent formability
- **Risk Score**: 15% (Low risk)

### 2. **HR Coils (Hot Rolled Coils)**
- **Thickness**: 1.2 - 12.7 mm
- **Width**: 600 - 1500 mm
- **Length**: Coil form
- **Density**: 7.85 g/cm³
- **Applications**: Structural, heavy machinery, construction
- **Quality**: Good strength, moderate surface finish
- **Risk Score**: 22% (Medium risk)

### 3. **Plates**
- **Thickness**: 3 - 100 mm
- **Width**: 1000 - 2000 mm
- **Length**: 2000 - 6000 mm
- **Density**: 7.85 g/cm³
- **Applications**: Boilers, pressure vessels, shipbuilding
- **Quality**: High strength, precision cut
- **Risk Score**: 28% (Medium-High risk)

### 4. **Sheets**
- **Thickness**: 0.4 - 2.0 mm
- **Width**: 800 - 1500 mm
- **Length**: 2000 - 4000 mm
- **Density**: 7.85 g/cm³
- **Applications**: Roofing, cladding, light fabrication
- **Quality**: Excellent finish, lightweight
- **Risk Score**: 18% (Low-Medium risk)

## Material Characteristics

### CR Coils
- **Best for**: Precision, high-quality applications
- **Advantages**: Excellent surface finish, tight tolerances
- **Handling**: Requires careful storage to prevent corrosion
- **Cost**: Premium pricing due to quality

### HR Coils
- **Best for**: Structural and general-purpose applications
- **Advantages**: Good strength-to-weight ratio, cost-effective
- **Handling**: Standard storage conditions
- **Cost**: Mid-range pricing

### Plates
- **Best for**: Heavy-duty, high-stress applications
- **Advantages**: Maximum strength, precision cutting available
- **Handling**: Requires heavy equipment for handling
- **Cost**: Premium pricing for thick plates

### Sheets
- **Best for**: Lightweight, aesthetic applications
- **Advantages**: Easy to work with, excellent finish
- **Handling**: Standard storage, prone to bending
- **Cost**: Competitive pricing

## Transportation Considerations

### Rail Transport (Optimal for Bokaro)
- **Best for**: CR Coils, HR Coils, Plates
- **Advantages**: Cost-effective for bulk, environmental benefits
- **Capacity**: 400-600 tonnes per rake
- **Cost Savings**: 15-30% vs road transport

### Road Transport (Last-mile)
- **Best for**: Sheets, small quantities
- **Advantages**: Flexibility, door-to-door delivery
- **Capacity**: 15-25 tonnes per truck
- **Use Case**: Final delivery to customers

### Hybrid Approach (Recommended)
1. **Rail**: Bokaro → Regional hubs (Kolkata, Patna, etc.)
2. **Road**: Regional hubs → Customer locations
3. **Result**: Optimal cost + delivery time
