## ðŸ” Analysis Methodology

### 1. **Multi-Scenario Analysis**
Evaluates multiple possible outcomes:
- **Recommended Scenario** - Best option based on data
- **Alternative Scenarios** - Other viable options
- **Risk Scenarios** - Potential negative outcomes

Each scenario includes:
- Probability of occurrence
- Expected costs and benefits
- Time requirements
- Risk levels
- Efficiency metrics

### 2. **Historical Pattern Matching**
Learns from past decisions:
- Success rate of similar decisions
- Average outcomes
- Number of past instances
- Trend analysis (improving/stable/declining)

### 3. **Risk Assessment & Mitigation**
Identifies and addresses risks:
- Risk level classification (Low/Medium/High/Critical)
- Key risk factors
- Mitigation strategies
- Contingency planning

### 4. **Cost-Benefit Analysis**
Evaluates financial impact:
- Implementation costs
- Expected benefits
- ROI calculations
- Margin improvements
- Revenue opportunities

### 5. **Confidence Scoring**
Provides reliability metrics:
- Overall confidence percentage
- Historical success rate
- Data quality assessment
- Uncertainty factors

### 6. **Alternative Comparison**
Evaluates multiple options:
- Pros and cons for each option
- Comparative scoring
- Trade-off analysis
- Recommendation ranking

### 7. **Outcome Prediction**
Predicts expected results:
- Best-case scenario
- Worst-case scenario
- Most-likely scenario
- Probability distribution
